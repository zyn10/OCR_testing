How to Extract Text from Image: Simple and Advanced OCR Methods

Learn how to extract text from image files easily. Discover the benefits it offers for both professionals and students.

Are you wondering how to extract text from an image but confused about where to get started? Imagine you're working on a research paper and need a quotation from a book, but you only have a picture of the book's page. What would you do in this situation? Typing all the text manually would take a lot of time and effort.

Fortunately, there's a faster and easier solution. With the right tools, you can extract text from an image in just seconds.

What is OCR (Optical Character Recognition)?

OCR is a technology that turns text in images into editable text. It helps computers read words from photos, screenshots, scanned pages, or PDFs.

Instead of seeing an image as just pixels, OCR recognizes letters, symbols, and numbers. Modern OCR tools are smart and accurate, even with different image qualities or fonts.

OCR technology is used in many fields like education, healthcare, and banking. It saves time and makes text easy to copy, edit, and store digitally.

How Does OCR Technology Work?

OCR doesn't work in a single step. It uses multiple stages to extract text from image with high accuracy.

1. Image Processing

Image processing is the first step. The system improves image quality by improving contrast, removing noise, and fixing skew. This step makes sure that text stands out clearly from the background.

2. Text Detection

Next is text detection, where the OCR engine identifies areas in the image that contain text. This is especially important for complex layouts like forms or magazines.

3. Character Recognition

Character recognition is next in line, where detected shapes are matched to known letters or numbers using neural networks or pattern recognition.

4. Post-Processing

Finally, post-processing is the last step that corrects errors using dictionaries, contextual understanding, and grammar rules. It dramatically improves readability and accuracy, especially for longer texts.

Methods to Extract Text from Image

Extracting text from images doesn't always require technical skills or expensive software. In fact, some of the best OCR methods are available online or even built into tools you may be using every day.

Below are some of the most popular and practical methods to extract text from images. These methods are effective, easy, and suitable for beginners and professionals.

1. Use an Online Image to Text Converter Tool

Image to text converter is a tool that extracts text from an image and converts it into editable text. There are many online image-to-text converter tools available that help you in the process.

What you need to do is simply search for "image to text converter" or "OCR tool" on Google. You will get multiple results on the screen.

How to use this tool?

The method to use this tool is very user-friendly and simple. Here are the steps:

  • Simply drag and drop your file or click to upload an image to extract text.
  • Once the file is uploaded, an "Extract Text" button will appear. Click the button to start the conversion process, and you will receive the results instantly.
  • Finally, the text from your image will be converted into machine-readable format. You can copy the text and use it wherever you want.

2. Extract Text from Image with Google Drive

With Google Drive, picture to text is just a matter of converting an image to Google Doc. This method uses built-in OCR technology to extract text from images.

To use this method, follow these steps:

  • Upload your file to Google Drive.
  • Open it with "Google Docs" through Google Drive.
  • The conversion process is started as the file loads.
  • You may need to proofread or edit the extracted text after conversion for better accuracy.

3. Convert Photo to Text in Microsoft Word

Converting an image to text in Microsoft Word takes a few additional steps, including converting the image to PDF, but the entire process can be done within MS Word. This method uses built-in OCR technology to convert images into editable text and can be processed offline.

To apply the procedure, follow these steps:

  • Copy your image into Word document.
  • Press F12 or go to File > Save as and choose PDF from the menu.
  • In Word, go to File > Open.
  • Select the PDF you just saved to open it in Word.
  • Click "OK" on the prompt about converting the PDF to editable text.

When you convert an image to PDF, it may open automatically in a PDF reader. At that point, your text has already been extracted.

Sometimes we need to extract text from images, but manually typing it wastes time and effort. With OCR tools, extracting text from images has become easier and faster than ever.

Whether you use online image to text converters, Google Drive, or Microsoft Word, these effective methods make text extraction simple, accessible, and accurate for both students and professionals.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I convert an image to text online for free?

You can convert an image to text using a free online OCR tool. The process is simple: upload your image file, click on the extract text button, and the tool will do the rest. Within seconds, you'll get editable text that you can copy or download.

Can Google extract text from images?

Yes, Google can extract text from images using Google Drive and Google Lens. You just need to upload the image file to Google Docs through Google Drive, or scan it with Google Lens to copy the text quickly and easily.

Is image to text conversion accurate?

Image to text conversion is mostly accurate when the image is clear. However, if the image is blurry or handwritten, then you may need to proofread extracted text again to solve some errors.

How does an online photo to text converter work?

An online photo to text converter uses OCR technology to scan and recognize text within an image. It detects letters and words, then converts them into editable text that you can copy, edit, or download.
