About Us

Image to Text is an online tool that uses OCR technology to recognize and extract text from images. It helps professionals, students, teachers, and researchers to easily convert text into an editable form with accuracy.

Image to Text was launched in 2023 with the goal of providing a simple and reliable OCR solution for converting images into text. Today, ImagetoText is one of the most visited OCR sites, trusted by users all over the world.
